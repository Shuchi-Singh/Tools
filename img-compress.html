How It Works

1. Upload Your Image

Drag and drop or select an image from your device. We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats.

2. Adjust Settings

Choose your preferred compression level, output format, and optional resizing.

3. Compress

Click the compress button and wait a few seconds for processing.

4. Download

Save your optimized image with reduced file size.

Why Compress Images?

  • Faster websites: Smaller images load faster, improving user experience
  • Better SEO: Page speed is a ranking factor for search engines
  • Reduced bandwidth: Save on hosting costs and data transfer
  • Email attachments: Send images more easily with smaller file sizes
  • Social media: Some platforms compress images automatically, so it's better to optimize first
